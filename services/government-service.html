GOVERNMENT SERVICE

At Inner Management Services, we understand the importance of a well maintained environment and how this can have a profound impact on your high profile facility. Having worked with state and local governments, we understand the responsibility that comes with servicing a secure space. Within this sector our team is familiar with providing the full range of facility services to offices, councils, libraries, schools, community halls, public toilets and amenities and Bus fleets. All our employees at education facilities have police clearance and working with children checks. Our staff are trained to be friendly, professional and efficient, so we can handle any type of maintenance or cleaning work in high-compliance settings.

Facilities we service:

  • Public toilets and amenities
  • Government own Energy supplier buildings
  • Community Halls and Community centres
  • Bus Fleets

To learn more about our cleaning service or discover how we could improve your workplace, contact us.
