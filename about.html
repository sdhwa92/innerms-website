WHO WE ARE

Inner Management Services is a cleaning company that works across most areas of commercial cleaning, including industrial buildings, commercial offices, shopping centres and government facilities. We also offer a range of specialised cleaning services and focused on providing owners and managers with general maintenance and service solutions that provide quality outcomes at a cost effective price.

We are proud to say that Inner Management Services valued employees share our commitment and pride in our high cleaning standard with safe work/training system. We are the company that completely understands that we are to complete our duties as per your specifications and conditions of contract at all times with a friendly professional nature.

Inner Management Services undertakes all the hard work to ensure that you are completely satisfied. Some of the benefits we provide include:

  • Fully trained and experienced supervisors and cleaners
  • Full security checks
  • The option of Weekly, Fortnightly or Monthly quality assurance audits
  • Customisable specifications
  • $20 million public liability cover
  • Communication books on each site that are checked and signed off after each clean
  • Uniformed and professional cleaning staff
  • Current and up to date Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) supplied and stored on site for each chemical used
  • Immediate reporting of any problems observed by cleaning staff, cleaning related or otherwise, to the contract manager

We aim to be competitive while providing professional, responsive, efficient and high quality services across a diverse range of commercial, domestic and public sector environments. Please get a free quote.
